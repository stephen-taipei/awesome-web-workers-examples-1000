About Polar Coordinates

Transform images between Cartesian (Rectangular) and Polar coordinates. This creates effects like "Little Planet" (Polar to Cartesian) or unrolling a circle (Cartesian to Polar).

Transformations:

Rectangular to Polar (Circle Swirl):
  x = center + r * cos(theta)
  y = center + r * sin(theta)
  where r maps to y_src, theta maps to x_src

Polar to Rectangular (Unroll):
  r = sqrt(dx^2 + dy^2)
  theta = atan2(dy, dx)
  x_src = map(theta, -PI..PI, 0..width)
  y_src = map(r, 0..radius, 0..height)
