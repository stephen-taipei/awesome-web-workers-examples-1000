About Watercolor Effect

Simulate a watercolor painting by smoothing areas of similar color while enhancing significant edges. This removes fine details and gives a "wash" appearance.

Processing Pipeline:

  1. Median Filter: Reduces noise and smooths textures while preserving edges.
  2. Edge Detection: Identifies outlines (Sobel/Canny).
  3. Blending: Combines the smoothed image with darkened edges to mimic paint pooling.
