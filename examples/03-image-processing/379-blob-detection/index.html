About Blob Detection

Detects regions in a digital image that differ in properties, such as brightness or color, compared to surrounding regions. This example uses a simplified Scale-Invariant Feature Transform (SIFT) approach using Laplacian of Gaussian (LoG) approximation (Difference of Gaussians).
