About Lens Correction

Correct lens distortions such as Barrel (fisheye-like) or Pincushion (inward squeezing) distortion. This is essential for correcting images taken with wide-angle lenses or action cameras.

Brown-Conrady Model (Simplified):

r_src = r_dest * (1 + k1 * r^2 + k2 * r^4)

k1, k2: Distortion coefficients
- Positive k: Corrects Pincushion (creates Barrel to compensate)
- Negative k: Corrects Barrel (creates Pincushion to compensate)
