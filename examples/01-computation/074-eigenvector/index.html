About Eigenvectors

For eigenvalue λ, eigenvector v satisfies Av = λv where v ≠ 0.

Algorithms

AlgorithmFindsConvergence
Power IterationDominant eigenvectorLinear

Properties

  • Eigenvectors for distinct eigenvalues are linearly independent
  • Symmetric matrices have orthogonal eigenvectors
  • Eigenvectors span the eigenspace for each eigenvalue
  • Normalization is arbitrary (any scalar multiple is valid)
