About Standard Deviation

Standard deviation measures the amount of variation or dispersion in a dataset.

Population vs Sample

Type Symbol Formula Divisor
Population σ (sigma) σ = √(Σ(x-μ)² / N) N
Sample s s = √(Σ(x-x̄)² / (n-1)) n-1

Welford's Algorithm

A numerically stable, single-pass algorithm that avoids catastrophic cancellation when computing variance.

Interpretation

  • ~68% of data within 1 std dev of mean (normal distribution)
  • ~95% of data within 2 std devs
  • ~99.7% of data within 3 std devs
