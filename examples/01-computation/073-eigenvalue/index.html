About Eigenvalues

For a square matrix A, eigenvalue λ satisfies Av = λv for some non-zero vector v.

Algorithms

AlgorithmFindsBest For
QR IterationAll eigenvaluesGeneral matrices
Power MethodLargest |λ|Dominant eigenvalue
Inverse IterationSmallest |λ|Near-singular matrices
Shifted QRAll eigenvaluesFaster convergence

Properties

  • n×n matrix has exactly n eigenvalues (counting multiplicity)
  • Symmetric matrices have real eigenvalues
  • det(A) = product of eigenvalues
  • trace(A) = sum of eigenvalues
