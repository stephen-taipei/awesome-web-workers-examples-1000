About Quartiles

Quartiles divide data into four equal parts. They're fundamental to understanding data distribution.

Definitions

Quartile Percentile Description
Q1 25th Lower quartile - 25% of data below
Q2 50th Median - middle value
Q3 75th Upper quartile - 75% of data below

IQR (Interquartile Range)

IQR = Q3 - Q1. Measures the spread of the middle 50% of data. Used for outlier detection:

  • Mild outlier: Below Q1 - 1.5×IQR or above Q3 + 1.5×IQR
  • Extreme outlier: Below Q1 - 3×IQR or above Q3 + 3×IQR
