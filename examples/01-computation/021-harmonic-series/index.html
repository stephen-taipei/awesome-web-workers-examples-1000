About Harmonic Series

The harmonic series is the sum: H(n) = 1 + 1/2 + 1/3 + ... + 1/n

Key Properties:

  • H(n) ≈ ln(n) + γ (Euler-Mascheroni constant γ ≈ 0.5772)
  • The series diverges (grows without bound)
  • Alternating harmonic series: 1 - 1/2 + 1/3 - ... = ln(2)
