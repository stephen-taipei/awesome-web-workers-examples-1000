About Laplace Transform

The Laplace Transform converts time-domain signals into the complex frequency domain, enabling analysis of linear time-invariant (LTI) systems.

Definition

F(s) = L{f(t)} = ∫₀^∞ f(t)e^(-st) dt

where s = σ + jω is the complex frequency

Common Transforms

f(t) F(s)
δ(t) (impulse) 1
u(t) (step) 1/s
e^(-at) 1/(s+a)
sin(ωt) ω/(s²+ω²)
