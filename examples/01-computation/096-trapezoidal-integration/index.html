About Trapezoidal Rule

The trapezoidal rule approximates the definite integral by summing areas of trapezoids under the curve.

Formula

∫f(x)dx ≈ h/2 × [f(a) + 2f(x₁) + 2f(x₂) + ... + 2f(x_{n-1}) + f(b)]

where h = (b-a)/n is the step size

Error Analysis

  • Doubling n: Reduces error by factor of 4
  • Best for: Smooth, non-oscillatory functions
  • Caution: Large errors for highly curved functions
