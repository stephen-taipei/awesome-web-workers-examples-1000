About Inverse FFT

The IFFT transforms frequency-domain data back to the time domain, enabling signal synthesis from spectral components.

IFFT Formula

x[n] = (1/N) Σₖ₌₀^(N-1) X[k] · e^(i·2πkn/N)
Note: Positive exponent, divided by N

Relationship to FFT

Conjugate Method
IFFT(X) = (1/N) · conj(FFT(conj(X)))
Direct Method
Same as FFT with +i instead of -i
Perfect Recovery
IFFT(FFT(x)) = x

Applications

  • Signal synthesis: Generate signals with specified frequency content
  • Filtering: Modify spectrum then IFFT to get filtered signal
  • Convolution: FFT → multiply → IFFT (fast convolution)
  • Audio effects: Spectral processing for music/speech
