About Spearman Rank Correlation

Spearman's ρ (rho) measures the strength and direction of monotonic relationships between two variables using their ranks instead of raw values.

Key Advantages

  • Works with ordinal data
  • Detects non-linear monotonic relationships
  • Robust to outliers
  • No assumption of normality

Formula

Without ties:

ρ = 1 - (6 × Σdᵢ²) / (n × (n² - 1))

With ties (Pearson on ranks):

ρ = Σ(Rₓ - R̄ₓ)(Rᵧ - R̄ᵧ) / √(Σ(Rₓ - R̄ₓ)² × Σ(Rᵧ - R̄ᵧ)²)

Interpretation

ρ Value Interpretation
+1.0 Perfect positive monotonic
+0.7 to +0.9 Strong positive
+0.4 to +0.6 Moderate positive
0 to +0.3 Weak/No relationship
-0.7 to -0.9 Strong negative
-1.0 Perfect negative monotonic
