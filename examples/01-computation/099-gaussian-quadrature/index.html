About Gaussian Quadrature

Gaussian quadrature achieves optimal accuracy by choosing both the evaluation points (nodes) and weights to exactly integrate polynomials of degree up to 2n-1.

Formula

∫₋₁¹ f(x)dx ≈ Σᵢ wᵢ · f(xᵢ)

where xᵢ are roots of Legendre polynomial Pₙ(x) and wᵢ are the corresponding weights

Properties

Why Gaussian Quadrature?

  • Optimal: Best accuracy for given number of function evaluations
  • Exponential convergence: For smooth functions
  • Few points: Often 5-10 points give excellent results
