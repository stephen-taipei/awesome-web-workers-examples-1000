About Moving Averages

Moving averages smooth time series data by creating a constantly updated average of past values.

Types of Moving Averages

Type Formula Characteristics
SMA Equal weights Simple, lags most, smoothest
EMA Exponential decay Fast response, good for trends
WMA Linear weights Balance of SMA and EMA

Common Applications

Field Use Case
Finance Stock price trend analysis, trading signals
Signal Processing Noise reduction, smoothing
Economics GDP, inflation smoothing
Sensors Temperature, accelerometer filtering
