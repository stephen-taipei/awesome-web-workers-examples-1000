About Kronecker Product

The Kronecker product A ⊗ B creates a block matrix where each element a_ij is replaced by a_ij × B.

Properties

  • Dimensions: (m×n) ⊗ (p×q) = (mp × nq)
  • Mixed-product: (A ⊗ B)(C ⊗ D) = (AC) ⊗ (BD)
  • Transpose: (A ⊗ B)^T = A^T ⊗ B^T
  • Trace: tr(A ⊗ B) = tr(A) × tr(B)

Kronecker Sum

A ⊕ B = A ⊗ I_n + I_m ⊗ B (for square matrices)

Applications

  • Quantum computing (tensor product of qubits)
  • Signal processing (2D filters)
  • Graph theory (Cartesian product)
  • Solving matrix equations (vec operator)
