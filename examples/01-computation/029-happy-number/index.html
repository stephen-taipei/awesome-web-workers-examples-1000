About Happy Numbers

A happy number reaches 1 when repeatedly replaced by the sum of squares of its digits.

Example: 19 → 1² + 9² = 82 → 64 + 4 = 68 → 100 → 1 (Happy!)

Unhappy (sad) numbers enter a cycle that never includes 1.
