About Linear Systems

Solve the system Ax = b where A is an n×n matrix and b is an n-vector.

Algorithms

AlgorithmTypeComplexityBest For
Gaussian EliminationDirectO(n³)General systems
LU DecompositionDirectO(n³)Multiple right-hand sides
JacobiIterativeO(n² per iter)Diagonally dominant
Gauss-SeidelIterativeO(n² per iter)Faster than Jacobi

Convergence

  • Direct methods: Always find solution (if exists)
  • Iterative methods: Require diagonally dominant or SPD matrices
  • Gauss-Seidel converges ~2× faster than Jacobi
