About Kendall Tau

Kendall's τ (tau) measures the ordinal association between two variables by counting concordant and discordant pairs.

Pair Types

Concordant

Both X and Y increase or both decrease

sign(Xⱼ-Xᵢ) = sign(Yⱼ-Yᵢ)
Discordant

One increases while the other decreases

sign(Xⱼ-Xᵢ) ≠ sign(Yⱼ-Yᵢ)
Tied

No difference in X, Y, or both

Xⱼ = Xᵢ or Yⱼ = Yᵢ

Tau Variants

Variant Formula Use Case
τ-a (C - D) / n(n-1)/2 No ties expected
τ-b (C - D) / √((n₀-n₁)(n₀-n₂)) Square tables with ties
τ-c 2m(C - D) / (n²(m-1)) Rectangular tables

Interpretation

τ Value Interpretation
+1.0 Perfect agreement
+0.5 to +0.9 Strong positive
+0.2 to +0.5 Moderate positive
-0.2 to +0.2 Weak/No association
-1.0 Perfect disagreement
